Hubert Kurkiewicz

(Sporo Wody)

multimedia artist, born in 97' in Słupsk, Poland. In art, he draws attention to the interpenetration of images and sounds, their coexistence, random interactions, relationships and the influence they have on each other.

Nostalgia and hypnagogy, are leitmotifs of Kurkiewicz's work, drawing from subconscious feelings and instincts.
